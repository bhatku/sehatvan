Sahaj Sehat - सहज सेहत

‘Sahaj Sehat’ series carries the viewpoints of how body’s auto-repair capabilities can make use of medicines redundant in lifestyle diseases.
‘सहज सेहत’ श्रंखला इस सोच को प्रस्तुत करती है कि कैसे शरीर की ऑटो-रिपेयर क्षमताओं से लाइफ़स्टाइल बीमारियों को हमेशा के लिए खत्म किया जा सके।



Dr Vipin Gupta is a drug discovery scientist. He spent over two decades developing new medicines for various pharma companies in India, Europe and America. In 2011 he was invited by The Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences at Nobel Museum, Stockholm to represent Southeast Asia. He co-founded a research publishing company ‘Inventi’ in 2010 and a self-healing space ‘Sehatvan’ in 2016. As of now he is actively engaged in discovering body’s auto-repair capabilities that would enable use of medicines redundant in lifestyle diseases.
